[Type Approval](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/vehicle-type-approval/)
We are the designated UK Type Approval Authority for automotive products and also a designated Technical Service for type approval testing in the United Nations (UN) scheme. VCA is also responsible for certification under UK type approval schemes.

![Image 5: GB Type Approval](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/GBType3.jpg)

[GB Type Approval](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/vehicle-type-approval/gb-type-approval-scheme/)
The GB type approval scheme is applicable for manufacturers wishing to place vehicles and components on the market in Great Britain.

[VCA's Self-Serve Digital Portal](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/vca-portal/)
Type Approval enquiries and applications for all schemes must now be made through our self-service portal.

Your career can help drive a safer, more environmentally friendly future. Make a difference to society by joining our dynamic, innovative and inclusive team.

[Conformity of Production (CoP)](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/conformity-of-production/)
Conformity of Production (CoP) is a means of evidencing the ability to produce a series of products that exactly match the specification, performance and marking requirements outlined in the type approval documentation.

![Image 12](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/wp-content/themes/vca/images/image-mask-home.png)

![Image 13: Connected and Automated Vehicles](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/connected-vehicles.jpg)

[Connected and Automated Vehicles](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/connected-and-automated-vehicles/)
By 2035, it is estimated that 40% of new car sales will have self-driving capability. To realise this ambition the trialling of this technology and the deployment into all types of vehicles in the marketplace has to be completed safely and securely.

![Image 14](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/wp-content/themes/vca/images/image-mask-home.png)

![Image 15: Electronic Certificates of Conformity (eCoCs)](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/wp-content/uploads/2022/05/vehicle-line-up.jpg)

[Electronic Certificates of Conformity (eCoCs)](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/electronic-certificates-of-conformity-ecocs/)
An initiative is underway to move from paper to electronic Certificates of Conformity (eCoCs) - a certificate issued by a manufacturer to certify that an individual vehicle has been produced in conformity with an approved type.

![Image 16](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/wp-content/themes/vca/images/image-mask-home.png)

![Image 17: Dangerous Goods](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/wp-content/uploads/2020/03/dangerous-goods.jpg)

[Dangerous Goods](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/dangerous-goods/)
Our Dangerous Goods Office is the UK authority for the certification of packaging and intermediate bulk containers used for the transport of dangerous goods, in accordance with national and international regulations.

![Image 18](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/wp-content/themes/vca/images/image-mask-home.png)

![Image 19: Fuel Consumption and CO<sub>2</sub>](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/cars-vans-emissions.jpg)

[Fuel Consumption and CO 2](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/fuel-consumption-co2/)
We have developed databases listing most of the cars and new vans available for sale in the UK. Follow the link below for more information on using the databases, and the other related services to industry offered by us.

![Image 20](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/wp-content/themes/vca/images/image-mask-home.png)

![Image 21: Other Certification](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/other_certification.jpg)

[Other Certification](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/other-certification/)
In addition to our Type Approval role, we carry out a number of additional certification activities. Learn more about these here.

![Image 22](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/wp-content/themes/vca/images/image-mask-home.png)

![Image 23: Vehicle Importing](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/wp-content/uploads/2020/03/vehicle-importing.jpg)

[Vehicle Importing](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/vehicle-importing/)
This service will be of interest to you if you're wishing to permanently import a car, motorcycle, or goods vehicle into the UK.

![Image 24](https://www.vehicle-certification-agency.gov.uk/wp-content/themes/vca/images/image-mask-home.png)

In addition to providing type approval technical and approval authority services, we can also provide copies of all automotive European Community and UN ECE legislation in a number of ways.
